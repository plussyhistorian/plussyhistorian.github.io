JoSniffy (Jo)

Jo (also known as Jo Sniffy) is one of the two founding members of Alpharad's second channel. Jo started the channel in May of 2019 alongside close friend Alpharad (Jacob).

Throughout fall of 2019, Jo was an English Teacher at a high school in Oklamhoma. Jo is widely known for his illiteracy.

Jo tragically passed away on August 11th, 2020. His memory and legacy continue to live on to this day.

In YouTube video THE FINAL SMASH DIRECT, from October 5th 2021, Jo reacted negatively to the reveal that Sora from Kingdom Hearts would be the final DLC fighter in Smash Bros ultimate.

At some point in 2025, Jo moved back to Oklamhoma to pursue his teaching career, marking the end of Alpharad's second channel
